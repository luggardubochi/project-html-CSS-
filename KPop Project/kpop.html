Popular Kpop Groups(Male)

BTS

BTS is a Korean boy band made up of seven members, Kim Namjoon(stage name: RM), Jeon Jungkook(stage name: Jungkook), Park Jimin(stage name:Jimin), Min Yoongi(stage name: Suga, AgustD), kim Seokjin(stage name: Jin), Kim Taehyung(stage name: V), Jeon Hoseok(stage name: J-hope). BTS stands for Bangtan Sonyeondan which means Bulletproof Boy Scouts in English. They are known for their hit singles "dynamite", "butter", "mic drop", "boy with luv", "sweat,blood and tears", "idol", "run". Their fandom name is "Army".

Stray KIds

Stray Kids is a Kpop boy band made up of eight members, Bang Chan, Lee Minho(stage name: Lee Know), Changbin, Han Jisung(stage name: Han), Hwang Hyunjin(stage name: Hyunjin), Felix, Seungmin and I.N, and a ninth member Woojin who left the group. They are known for their hit singles "lalalala", "chk chk boom", "christmas evel", "case 143", "God's Menu", "S-class", "jjam". Their fandom name is "Stay".

Enhypen

Enhypen is a famous korean boy band consists of seven members, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-Ki. Theya re known for their hit singles "bite me", "XO" and "Brought the heat back". Their fandom name is "Engene".

Exo

This Korean boy band consists of nine members, Kai, Baek Hyun, Sunho, Chanyeol, Sehun, Huang Zitao, Kris Wu, D.O, Chen, Lu Han, Xlumin, Lay Zhang. They are known for their hit singles, "Love Shot", "Growl", "Ko KO Bop", "The First Snow". Their fandom name is "Exo-L".

Popular Kpop Groups(Female)

Blackpink

Blackpink is a popular Korean girl band, made up of four members, Jennie Kim(stage name: Jennie), Lisa, Rose(pronounced: Rosaay), and Jisoo. They are popularly known for their hit singles, "bam bam bam", "How you like that", "du du du du", "pink venom", "kill this love". Their fandom name is "blink".

G-idle

G-idle is a Korean girl band made up of six members, Yuqi, Minnie, Cho Mi-yeon, Yeh Shu Hua, Jeon So-yeon, Seo Soo-jin. They are famous for their hit singles, "HIWAA", "Villian dies", "Hann(Alone)", "Latata". Their fandom name is Neverland.

Twice

Twice is a famous Korean girl pop band made up of nine members, Momo, Minatozaki Sana, Tzuyu, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Jihyo, Mina, Chaeyoung, Dahyun. They are known for their hit singles, "Twice song", "More & More", "Likey", "Fancy". Their fandom name is "once".

Aespa

This is a famous Korean girl band, which consists of four members, Karina, Winter, Ningning, Giselle. They are known for their hit singles "next level", "hold on tight", "black mamba", "aenergy". Their fandom name is "MY".
