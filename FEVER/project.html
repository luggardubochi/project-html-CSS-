About Us

Hyatt hotel in collaboration with Arab Emirates, is known for genuine services and care to guests. Hyatt hotel is a four star hotel, and we aim at the best as we hope to give you an ambience and care that is considered best, by whosoever steps into Hyatt hotel. Hyatt Hotel was founded in the year, 1998. Then, it was just an inn, known as Hyatt's inn. Hyatt became a hotel in the year 2010. And it was awarded a four star hotel in UAE, in the year, 2018.

Unlike some other resting places. Hyatt Hotel ensure utmost security to their guest and we might as well say that to get into Hyatt hotel is a tough nut to crack. Hyatt Hotel pride itself on the ability to give customers security, care and provide other recreational facilities at customer's disposal. A shocking detail, Hyatt is a four star hotel but is relatively cheap. We carry out monthly discount taking off a percentage of charges, not only to our customers, but guests also. Hyatt does not fear bankruptcy, but its major goal is to offer an expensive service at a cheap rate, and put a smile on guest's faces.
